Video Game Courses

I've started giving video game courses in February 2018, at the Atelier du jeu vidéo in Angoulême. The class is composed of 9 pupils from 12 to 20 years old. I teach them the basics of Game Design, and how to create a game with Unity. Sometime I also talk about the industry, the different jobs and their role in the game production etc. We'll work with Unreal Engine from January 2019 until March 2019.
