Space Odd E.T.

Posted on December 05, 2018 · minutes read

Space Odd E.T. was made during the Game Maker's Toolkit Jam. The theme was GENRE, but you can’t MECHANIC. We (team of 5) choose to make a platformer without gravity. You control a little robot lost in space, trying to reach radio towers by jumping from platform to platform.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_-bA1ZQWhUU

I mainly worked on designing and implementing gameplay mechanics, but also on level design and visual/audio polishing.
