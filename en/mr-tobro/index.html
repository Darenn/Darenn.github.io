Posted on December 06, 2018 · minutes read

BUILD TURRETS. KILL VIRUSES. PROTECT THE CORE. EASY.

Your computer is being hacked by vicious virus ! It's the eeeeeeeeeend...
No ! Because Mr.Tobro antivirus is here to save YOUR system  !
At first, you'll be tight in Mr.Tobro small window. But the more you kill viruses, the greater it becomes !

Mr.Tobro is a Shooter Tower Defence where tower don't shoot automatically and you run out of space quickly.

https://youtu.be/tG45WDfIQds?t=134

Jupiter Hadley playing Mr.Tobro (start at 2:15)

I submitted this game to Ludum Dare #42 and LOWREZJAM.
I had to create a game with the theme "Running out of space" and with  a 64x64 resolution. The constraint fits perfectly the theme,  I literally ran out of space making this game. Moreover the main mechanic is about building turrets on the few available spaces you got, the more you build, the more you run out of space... It was my first time time using pico-8, and it was awesome.
