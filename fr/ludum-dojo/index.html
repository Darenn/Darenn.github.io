Ludum Dojo

All links to products are affiliated link, you pay the same price and I get a small percentage, thanks for your support!

Posted on December 03, 2018 · minutes read

Ludum Dojo is a kind of QTE Action Game I created for the Ludum Dare 37. The theme was One Room. When I started the jam, I had just came back from my Kendo course, that explains the idea. The player is a kendo-ka practicing with his master. The player has to push buttons on the right moment to counter the master's attacks.

My goal here as game developer, was to make 3D model, rig it, and animate it, in order to better understand the work of a 3D artist. The character isn't very pretty I know, but it works well.

The mood of the game was particularly appreciated. You can play the game on its page here !
